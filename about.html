Who Am I

I'm William, a cybersecurity enthusiast and ethical hacker on a mission to make the digital world a safer place. By day, I dissect malware and hunt vulnerabilities. By night, I document my findings so you don't have to learn the hard way.

My philosophy is simple: "I Hack So You Don't Have To" — I break things intentionally so you can understand how to protect them.

The Battlefield

Like the knight in the animation above, I fight daily battles against digital threats. From penetration testing and vulnerability research to malware analysis and forensics — every exploit I discover is another virus slashed from the system.

This blog serves as my battlefield log, documenting CTF walkthroughs, security tools, and defensive strategies.
